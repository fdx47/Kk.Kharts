The Poppins font family should be downloaded from Google Fonts:
https://fonts.google.com/specimen/Poppins

Download and place the following files in this folder:
- Poppins-Regular.ttf
- Poppins-SemiBold.ttf
- Poppins-Bold.ttf

Also include OpenSans fonts:
- OpenSans-Regular.ttf
- OpenSans-Semibold.ttf
